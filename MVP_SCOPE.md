# ShireProof MVP Scope

ShireProof is a field reporting app for companies with technicians.

## First Product Goal

Replace scattered paper forms, phone photos, and notes app logs with one centralized workflow:

1. A technician logs in.
2. The technician creates or opens a job report.
3. The technician adds job details, notes, checklist answers, and photos.
4. A manager reviews submitted reports from the company dashboard.

## First User Roles

- Owner: manages the company account.
- Manager: reviews reports and manages jobs.
- Technician: creates and submits reports.

## First App Screens

- Login
- Dashboard
- Reports list
- Create report
- Report detail
- Settings later

## Not In The First Build

- Billing
- Self-serve company signup
- PDF exports
- Customer portals
- Native mobile apps
- Complex scheduling

Those can come after the core reporting workflow feels useful.
